# Help Desk Concepts

## Topic
Help desk roles and workflows

## Key Points
- Ticketing system 
- Queue management
- SLAs and KPIs

## Explanation

### Ticketing system
A system which lets users to report problems to IT support. It helps by tracking and managing issues until they are resolved.

### Queue management
This is how IT helpdesk manage tickets. Resolving issues depending on its priority level (high, medium, low).

### SLAs and KPIs
SLA (Service Level Agreements) - Defines the amount of time a help desk team has to respond to and resolve a customer issue based on its priority level.
KPI (Key Performance Indicators) - Helps helpdesk teams measure the efficiency and effectiveness of their ticket management process. 

## Summary
Help desk roles and workflows are centered around resolving user issues by utilizing the ticketing system, managing tickets by priority, and maintaining clear communication with the users.
